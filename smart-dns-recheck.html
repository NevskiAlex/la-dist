How to watch after setup?

Access streaming services through their websites or dedicated apps

Streaming on website

If you don't have the app installed, we recommend streaming on the website. Click the link below to open the streaming service and start watching.

  • ITV.com

Streaming in app

If you already have the streaming service's app installed, just open it and start streaming. If not, visit the app store and install it.

  • BBC
  • ITV
  • Channel5
  • Channel4
If your Apple or Google account is set to a region where the streaming service app isn't supported, you may not be able to find it in the app store. You'll need to change your account's region. Learn how to do this.
